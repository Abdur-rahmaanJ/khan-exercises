Fill in the blank.

\large{N1} ____ \large{N2}

SOLUTION

  • <
  • >
\large{<} means "less than".
\large{>} means "greater than".

\large{\blue{UNIT}.\green{D1}} represents \large{\blue{UNIT \text{ units}} + \green{D1 \text{ hundreths}}}

\large{\blue{UNIT}.\green{D2}} represents \large{\blue{UNIT \text{ units}} + \green{D2 \text{ hundreths}}}

\large{\green{D1 \text{ hundreths}}} is more than \large{\green{D2 \text{ hundreths}}}.

\large{\green{D1 \text{ hundreths}}} is less than \large{\green{D2 \text{ hundreths}}}.

Therefore \large{N1} > \large{N2}.

Therefore \large{N1} < \large{N2}.
