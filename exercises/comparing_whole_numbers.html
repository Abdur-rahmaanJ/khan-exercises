\large{>} means "greater than".

\large{N1} is bigger than \large{N2} because it has more hundreds.

\large{N1} is smaller than \large{N2} because it has fewer hundreds.

\large{N1} is bigger than \large{N2} because it has more tens.

\large{N1} is smaller than \large{N2} because it has fewer tens.
