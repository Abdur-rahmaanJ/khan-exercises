In the equation, y_1 is equal to PRETTY_Y1, so the vertex of the parabola will have y-coordinate of PRETTY_Y1.

In the equation, the leading coefficient, a, is equal to PRETTY_A.

a is the reciprocal of 2 times the distance between the directrix to the focus. Therefore, the focus is 1 / (2 * A) units above the directrix.

a is the reciprocal of 2 times the distance between the directrix to the focus. Therefore, the focus is 1 / (2 * -A) units below the directrix.
